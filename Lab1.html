The Invisible Man

The Invisible Man is a classic science fiction novel written by H.G. Wells. It tells the story of a scientist who discovers the secret to invisibility but struggles with its consequences.

Reason why I love this book:

  1. The suspenseful and thrilling storyline.
  2. The intriguing character of the Invisible Man.
  3. The imaginative and thought-provoking science fiction ideas.

"The most wonderful thing about science is the power to change the world".
